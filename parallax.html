History of Charlotte

Charlotte was founded in 1775 and was given the nickname "The Queen City" because of the city being named after Queen Charlotte. With the county being named "Mecklenburg County" after the birthplace of the queen. On may 20th 1775, Charlotte had their own Declaration of Indepdence!.
